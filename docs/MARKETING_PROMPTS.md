# 🎨 Vertex Sentinel: Marketing Image Prompts

- **Core Palette:** Obsidian (#0B0E14), Electric Cyan (#00E5FF), Deep Purple (#7C3AED), Emerald Green (#10B981).
- **Style:** Glassmorphism, high-contrast neon, tech-minimalist, "Fail-Closed" security.

## 💡 Usage Tips
- **Aspect Ratios:** Adjust the `--ar` parameter based on your target platform (e.g., `16:9` for YouTube/Web, `1:1` for Instagram).
- **Brand Consistency:** Always include "Obsidian," "Cyan," and "Purple" in your color descriptors to maintain the Sentinel brand feel.
- **Negative Prompts:** If using Stable Diffusion, use: `bright colors, daylight, messy, cluttered, low resolution, blurry, text, watermark.`
